Overview

Top Bar Header

My Bazar has nice top bar section at the top of the site. It has contact number, order tracking, currency selection and language selection box. Though we have only one language in this version, we are going to make it multi language very soon.

Changing the currency will change the currency symbol and convert the product price as the exchange rate. You can always add/modify/remove any currency you want.

Middle Bar Header

The middle bar in header contains the company logo at the left side. At the right side it has an user menu, wishlist icon and the cart icon. These icons show the quantity of items they has.

Click on each icons to see the result

Menu Bar

My Bazar has menu bar with both categories and pages. Click on "All Category" to see the category list. At the right side of menu bar there are product pages.

Set categories from admin panel to show in category menu.

Banner

At the top side there is banner section which contain category menu at left, slider in middle and a promotion section at the right side. Banner can be use for site advertisements or promote any specific products, category of brands.

Category list will display as like as the category menu bar. Sliders and promotion images can be change from admin panel.

Category Collection

The home page has a well designed category collection section. This is a short way to visit a category quickly.

Select categories from admin panel to show in this section.

Deal of the week

This section shows weekly best sellers, featured products, trending products, new items etc. Show the best relevant product to customer for better experience.

Brands

All well known brands and partners of this market place will be here. It has a elegant animated design.

Authentication

My Bazar has a very easy customer registration process. A customer can sign up by filling only one form with minimum fields. A registered use will have access to all features of a customer. Just sign up in few seconds and ready dive in.

The customer must enter unique email, phone and username. Later on a customer can login with username or email.

The login settings can be found in app/Http/Controllers/Frontend/Auth/LoginController. Change login system whenever you want as your needs.

Warning! Please, change only if you know what you are doing.

If anybody forget him/her password, don't fret here is a password reset option. Click on the password reset from login or register page. Then it will redirect to this page below.

Enter email and hit the "Send Email Verification" Button. An email will sent to customer and redirect to password reset page.

If the page not redirected correctly or you have plan to reset password after a while the email has a password reset page link. Though the verification code will expire in 60 minutes.

It has a gorgeous admin login page designed. You can redirect to any panel from here.

Add to Cart

As all eCommerce we have a add to cart button in with each product. This button works with ajax, that means the page will never redirect while adding product to cart. So you can continue shopping, then checkout later on in the process. There are several ways you can add a product to cart. Individual product has a add to cart button, all product card has this button, sometime you may need to hover on the product.



It will add product to cart cookie. The cart cookie will expire in 2 hours. If you want to purchase something later then add the product to wishlist.

After successfully adding product to your cart the cart page will look like this. Click on the Checkout button from cart page to complete your order.

Shipping Address & Billing Address

The “shipping address” is the address to which a package will be shipped. It should not be confused with the billing address, which is the address of the person who is paying.

When you make a purchase, you will need to give two different addresses. The first one is the shipping address. This is information for the postman, he needs to know where he will be giving the package to.

The second address is the billing address. This is where the person paying for it lives, or at least, where the banks think they live.

Although there are some exceptions, most of the time, the billing address will be the same as the shipping address. Because most people get deliveries for themselves in their house.

Payment Method

Currently we have two payment methods "Credit Card" and "Cash on Delivery". Cash on delivery (COD) is a type of transaction where the recipient pays for a good at the time of delivery rather than using credit. Customer can also pay by electronic payment system which called credit card. We used stripe for credit card payment. Stripe supports processing payments in 135+ currencies, but some card brand networks have limitations on supported currencies that charges can be made with.

User Avatar

The avatar is the graphical representation of the customer, upload/change avatar by clicking the avatar area then click on "Update Image" button. The preferred image dimension is 225px * 225px with this size the image will have a good visual effect.

Personal Information

A customer's personal information will be kept by the system admin. These information will not share with any other person inside or outside of this site.

Default Shipping Address & Default Billing Address

A customer can store a default shipping address, it will automatically added to checkout page while ordering products. Though the customer can change these default address while checking out. Default address can be change anytime from the profile page.

Listen! Changing default shipping or billing address won't effect previously ordered information.
